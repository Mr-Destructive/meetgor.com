My thoughts on [The machines didn’t take your craft](https://www.davidabram.dev/musings/the-machine-didnt-take-your-craft/): The machines didn’t take your craft

## Commentary

- The machines didn’t take your craft
- A good one, the last paragraph hits home.No matter what tools arrive, no matter how powerful they become, they will always remain tools. They won’t replace our reason nor values. You will still choose what is worth building. And as long you reason, nothing essential has been lost.
- This is a good call to not abandon your craft, just because a faster method exist, never attach yourself to the tools. They will keep on changing.
